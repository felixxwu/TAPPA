Privacy Policy for TAPPA

Last updated: 10 July 2026

This privacy policy applies to the TAPPA game ("the game"), a single-player rally driving game published on Google Play and itch.io.

Data we collect

The game does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. It contains no analytics, no advertising, no third-party SDKs, no user accounts, and makes no network requests to any server that we control.

Data stored on your device

The game saves your progress — such as owned cars, upgrades, and completed rallies — locally on your device (in a file named profile.json). This information never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or to any third party. Uninstalling the game removes this data.

Children

The game does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this same URL with a revised "Last updated" date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact the developer at wufodnb@gmail.com.
